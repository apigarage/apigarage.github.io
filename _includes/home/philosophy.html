The Garage Philosophy

Don't Build It, Until You Need it.

We research your problem, and make sure it's totally worth solving the problem using technology. If there is perfectly matching off-the-shelf solution is available, we direct you in that direction.

Your Problem Becomes our Problem.

We don't only put ourselves in your shoes, but walk a mile with it. That gives us full understanding of your problem, and allows us to prescrive best solution.

Extensibility Over Scalability

We believe technology should foster business experiments and adapt new business requiements extremely fast. That's trully possible only with an extensible solution.
